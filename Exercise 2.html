Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière

by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

         

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Rivère was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of three portraits of the Rivère family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimension: 100 x 70 cm
Home: Musèe dù Louvre, Paris
